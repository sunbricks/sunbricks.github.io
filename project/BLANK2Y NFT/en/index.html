ABOUT BLANK2Y

BLANK2Y is a multinational boy group consisting of Youngbin, Louis,
Donghyuk, Siwoo, Mikey, Sungjun, DK, U, and Sodam.
A portmanteau of “blank” and “key” to signify “the key to opening blank spaces”, BLANK2Y is striving significantly to work as an honorary ambassador for the Jeju Tourism Association and its festival that focuses on
sending a message of hope and healing, “Blossom The Hope 2022.”

THIS KEY IS FROM SANTA

As time approaches the 24th- the release date of BLANK2Y’s first mini album,“K2Y I : CONFIDENCE Thumbs Up”- expectations are rising as to wonder what kind of music and performancethe group will present as phenomenal rookies. As ambassador in “Blossom The Hope,” a festival that partnered with COEX to provide healing in the midst of urban life during the 15th World Forestry Congress, BLANK2Y has broadcasted a strong message about healing from the tiring everyday life.

The upcoming album allows BLANK2Y’s nine different charms to shine through at once via their collaboration with graffiti artist Santa, who has worked on many other artists’ album art to magnify each musician’s own color. Feel BLANK2Y’s various charms glamorously reborn in the hands of a 1st generation Korean graffiti artist.

ABOUT ARTIST

Even in the 1990s, when graffiti was unheard of among Korean culture genres, Santa has led the frontlines of graffiti art to the present as a consistent, 1st generation Korean graffiti artist and designer.
Other than their creative artwork, Santa is a versatile multitalent in industrial design such as fashion and interior, as well as personally managing and creating characters, 3D art, logos, Flash, personal websites, shopping sites, and print work
